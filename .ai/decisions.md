# Decisions

Record important decisions here. Do not delete historical decisions unless they are clearly wrong or superseded.

## Decision template

```md
## YYYY-MM-DD — Decision title

Status: Proposed / Accepted / Superseded

Context:
- What problem or choice caused this decision?

Decision:
- What was decided?

Reason:
- Why was this chosen?

Impact:
- What files, modules, users, or future decisions does this affect?

Tradeoffs:
- What are we giving up?

Revisit when:
- What condition should make us reconsider?
```
